About

Tours is an established bed and breakfast with a four star rating. We are situated in Mumbai, the home of the Great White Shark. Your room leads out to a balcony overlooking the Walker Bay Whale Nature Reserve.

Here you can make yourself feel at home and enjoy all the sea has to offer. A short walk from your room will take you to the sea where you can take a breath-taking walk along the coast. In the one direction you will find the harbour which you can explore. Don't forget to try the fish and chips. In the other direction you will find a pleasant beach where you can take a swim or work on your tan. You might get lucky and see whales playing up close.

Back at Tours you can relax in the guest area with a few drinks and watch the sun set over the sea while braaing a steak on the coals. It is a good time to connect with family and friends back home over our super fast wifi network.
